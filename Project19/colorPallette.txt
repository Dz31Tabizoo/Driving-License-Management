Color	Usage- Examples
_______________________


Background (#092327)	The BackColor of your main Form.

Surface (#1E282A)	The BackColor of Panels, GroupBoxes, DataGridView, and TabPage.

Surface Border (#37474F)	DataGridView.GridColor, Panel.BorderColor (if using a custom border).
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Text White (#FFFFFF)	ForeColor for most controls placed on Background or Surface.

Text Secondary (#B0BEC5)	ForeColor for labels that are less important (e.g., "Hint:", watermarks).
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Primary (#00B8AD)	Primary buttons, active tabs, highlights, hyperlinks.

Primary Dark (#0B5351)	Hover state for primary buttons, secondary elements.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Accent (#FF6B6B)	Save, Submit, key metrics. The most important action on a screen.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Success (#69F0AE)	Success messages, positive trends.

Warning (#FFD740)	Warning messages, inactive states.

Error (#FF5252)	Error messages, Delete buttons, negative trends.
